  • Predicting Well Switching Behavior In Rural Bangladesh

    • Co-authored a report focused on predicting whether households in Bangladesh would switch from using contaminated wells to safe ones. The aim was to determine future policies for specific areas in Bangladesh as the rural population would continue relying primarily on tubewells for at least another decade. The study hoped that government welfare agencies could use the results to target households unlikely to switch to safe wells with tailored campaigns or incentives.
